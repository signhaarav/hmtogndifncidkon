Pyaare Musa aur Masi,

Aap dono ko is khoobsaut saalgirah ke mauke par dil se badhai! Saal bhar ki yeh journey aapke pyaar, samjhauta aur sath nibhane ki misaal hai.

Jab bhi main aap dono ko saath dekhta hoon, ek saccha pyaar nazar aata hai. Aapke rishte ki gehrai, aapki muskan aur aapka ek dusre ke liye samarpann bhavna sabko pyaar ka sabak sikhati hai.

Aap dono ka sath hamesha yunhi bana rahe, pyaar aur apnapan ka yeh rishta hamesha barhta rahe. Khushiyan aapke kadam chume aur har mushkil mein aap ek dusre ka sath den.

Dua karte hain ki aapka yeh pyaar ka safar hamesha khushhaal rahe, jisme har din naye rang bhar de aur har saal ek nayi khushi le kar aaye.
